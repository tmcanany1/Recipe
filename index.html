Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni pasta drizzled with a cheddar cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 box of macaroni noodles
    • 1 packet of cheese mix
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • a splash of milk
  • 6 cups of water

Directions

  1. Heat water in a pot.
  2. Once boiling pour macaroni in the pot.
  3. Boil noodles until soft.
  4. Drain water off once done.
  5. Put noodles back in pot.
  6. Pour cheese mix in pot.
  7. Put 4 tablespoons of butter in.
  8. Pour as much milk as necessary.
  9. Enjoy!
